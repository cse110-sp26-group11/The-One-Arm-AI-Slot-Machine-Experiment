The slot machine should make fun of AI, as in you are winning tokens and spending tokens." We went through each one and picked the best 5, and even though they technically worked, they had their own array of issues. We decided to refine them by prompting gemini to essential features like informing users about payouts, ability to add more tokens, choose the gamble amount, and just make it funnier and more fun. Everything else should've remained the same. After running the refinment prompt we picked the 3 best in accordance to our rubric. Improvements were subtle, but there were still issues. Some added new features that weren't asked for such as sound effects to the site. Another ended up breaking because the wheel spinner would stop in between emojis instead of cleanly on one. The quality of the code and style remained the same across all the improvements. We refined them again by asking for an odds adjustment feature, sound effects, and to be more fun. But virtually everything else should've remained the same. One of the builds ended up breaking after this refinement, so we proceeded with the two that didn't.
